#Webfont:
According website: https://www.editorx.com/shaping-design/article/font-size
, this is a list size of font for our webpage:
1. Desktop:
- Body text: 16px - 18px (1em - 1.125em)
- Headings: 1.96 time larger than body text:
	Body text(px)	Headings(px)
	16		31.36 - 32
	18		35 - 36
- Subheadings: smaller than heading. Depend to our heading size.
- Input field: match the body text = 1em.
2. Mobile:
- Body text: 14px - 16px (1em - 0.875em)
- Headings: 1.3 time larger than body text:
	Body text(px)	Headings(px)
	14		18
	16		21
- Subheadings: smaller than heading. If headings have value of 21px, we will choose the size of subheadings in range of (16px - 18px), and it will also lighter than font-weight of headings.
- Input field: match the body text = 1em.
